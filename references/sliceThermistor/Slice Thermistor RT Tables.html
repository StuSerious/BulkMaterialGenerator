300°C Thermistor
2
WARNING
3
Use of Slice Engineering's 300°C Thermistor requires firmware modification. Improperly configured firmware can cause a fire!

Marlin and Duet3D® firmware support the Slice Engineering® 300°C Thermistor. In your firmware settings, choose the 100K thermistor - ATC Semitec 104NT-4-R025H42G. Refer to documentation at marlinfw.org or configurator.reprapfirmware.org to configure your firmware PRIOR TO USE. DO NOT defeat the thermal runaway protection in your firmware. This will void any and all warranties.

If your printer uses an older version of open source firmware, use the Resistance-Temperature Table below.
